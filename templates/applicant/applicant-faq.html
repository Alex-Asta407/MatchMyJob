How is my resume score calculated?

  • Skills: 25% — Points for having skills, more for 5+ and 10+ skills.
  • Experience: 25% — Points for having experience, more for 2+ and 3+ jobs, and for detailed descriptions.
  • Education: 25% — Points for having education, more for 2+ and 3+ entries, and for detailed entries.
  • Contact & Additional Info: 25% — Points for having contact info, resume length, and common contact indicators (email, phone, LinkedIn, etc.).

The total score is capped at 100%. Hover over the percentage on your profile to see a qualitative label.

Resume Completeness & Match Percentage Labels:
  • Excellent: 80–100%
  • Good: 60–79%
  • Average: 40–59%
  • Needs Improvement: below 40%

These labels appear as tooltips when you hover over the score or match percentage in the app.

Other Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can I improve my resume score? — Add more relevant skills, detailed experience, and complete your education and contact info.
  • Why is my education or experience missing? — Make sure your resume uses clear section headers and standard formatting.
  • Who can see my resume? — Only you and employers you apply to can view your resume.
